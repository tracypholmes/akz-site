Since 2010, Zeta has raised over $2 million dollars for March of Dimes!

The 2016 campaign contributed in a big way to this total. In 2016, Zeta’s March for Babies national campaign kicked off the year with a goal to raise $460,000. By year’s end, our total was $481,353, which enabled Zeta to retain the title as MOD’s Top National Adult Service Partner and win the Alpha-Zeta Challenge!

This year, Zeta Phi Beta announced its national campaign theme:“45 Years of Service with MOD” to raise more than $500,000 to support the March of Dimes mission. Mary Breaux Wright, International President for Zeta Phi Beta Sorority states that, “2017 marks a significant and momentous milestone for Zeta Phi Beta Sorority and the March of Dimes. Forty-five years ago, the Sisterhood voted to adopt the March of Dimes as its official national partner – a kinship that has celebrated breakthroughs and successes in tackling the serious health crisis of prematurity for more than four decades.
